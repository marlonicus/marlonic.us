About Me

I am a developer with an unquenchable thirst for making enjoyable digital experiences and exploring new technology. I am most proficient with web tools and will usually reach for JavaScript to get an idea going. I have several years of React experience under my belt, preceded by a few more years with other JS frameworks and libraries.

I love the regular leaps and bounds forward the web platform takes, and enjoy keeping up to date with the latest news and trends surrounding it. I have been passionate about technology since I was old enough to hold a mouse, and began my career as a Flash developer when I hit 16; I haven't looked back since.

Beyond the code and pixels, I also enjoy taking a more holistic view of the value my team is trying to deliver - the goals we strive to reach and the approaches we take to get there. I believe in agile principles and lean processes; I don't believe in any silver bullets. I believe that in order to achieve a teams full potential they must feel empowered and safe enough to regularly innovate on their ways of working.

In my spare time you can usually find me infatuated with a new hobby or interest. Currently I'm very much into climbing and VR/3D. I'm also an avid gamer and music lover, with a unique ability to empty an entire room with a guitar in my hand.
